About Us

Welcome to viator, your number one source for all laptop computers. We're dedicated to giving you the very best of our products, with a focus on dependability, customer service and uniqueness. Founded in 2018 by George Steive, Viator has come a long way from its beginnings in a small building. When Viator first started out, its passion for latest technology drove it to do intense research, and gave it the impetus to turn hard work and inspiration into to a booming company. We now serve customers all over the world, and are thrilled to be a part of the eco-friendly, wing of the IT industry. We hope you enjoy our products as much as we enjoy offering them to you. If you have any questions or comments, please don't hesitate to contact us.
